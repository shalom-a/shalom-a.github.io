Graze Analytics

March 2021 - May 2021 Company: Graze Analytics Software: Figma Developed Skill(s): Rapid Prototyping
Project Description
  • Problem: Resturants struggle to get the writtern information they need from their custormers to make their store better.
  • Solution: By changing the interface and adding leading questions, we were able to create a more informative app for the resturants.
  • Users: Iowa Resturants and customers

Testing & Prototyping

The first three rounds of testing were based on sketches of the design. The sketches were created in Microsoft One Note. With these prototypes, we created a simulation of what interacting with those pages on a the computer. That simulation was then presented to similar users. Using the information from the three rounds of user testing, we then created a higher fidelity prototype with Figma and did two rounds of testing with that.

Why the Design?

Based on user testing, people were more likely to write a review if they are prompted to do so. By adding questions based on how they respond with the emojis, the users would put even a few words. The client, resturant owners and managers, appreciated even simple responses like salty, cold, and too spicy.
